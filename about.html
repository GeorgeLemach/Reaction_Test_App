ABOUT REACTION TIME

Reaction time is the time taken by human or any system to respond to a given stimulus or event.

Reaction time of human is the time taken by us to react when we see something changing.

Average Reaction time of human is 220ms-250ms.

Reaction Time comes into play in fps games where even a difference of millisecond can cost you a round.
